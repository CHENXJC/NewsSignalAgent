# Product Plan

## Positioning

NewsSignalAgent is a local-first decision-support workflow that turns regional news inputs into business, market, policy, risk, and content signals. Its value is transparent prioritisation and comparison rather than article summarisation.

## Target users

- Students and early-career analysts building commercial awareness
- Small-business and marketing teams scanning for customer or workflow changes
- Creators seeking evidence-led content angles
- Portfolio reviewers assessing modular AI-agent product thinking

## MVP scope

The product now includes local demo data, a bounded official-feed pilot, configurable source metadata, rule-based article scoring, actionable insights, cross-country framing comparison, exports, and tests.

## NEWS-005 milestone

NEWS-005 adds a neutral Narrative Gap Detector. It compares regional/source-group emphasis for shared topics and turns differences into business, risk, and content hypotheses. This improves portfolio value by demonstrating explainable analytical product design beyond summarization while avoiding political truth judgments.

## Future scope

NEWS-006 will polish the dashboard and prepare a screenshot/public showcase pack. Later phases may add structured LLM analysis with schemas, human feedback, evaluation, history, and watchlists.

## GitHub showcase plan

Publish code, configs, original sample data, tests, documentation, safe screenshots, and clear setup instructions. Keep credentials, private feeds, paid text, local environments, and generated user reports out of Git.
